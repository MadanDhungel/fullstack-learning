Lets learn about Nepal

Mountains

The Himalayas are the highest mountain range in the world, home to Mount Everest, the tallest peak on Earth.

Rivers and Lakes

Nepal is home to many beautiful rivers and lakes, including the sacred Bagmati River and the stunning Phewa Lake.

Culture and Traditions

Nepal has a rich cultural heritage with diverse traditions, festivals, and cuisines that reflect its multi-ethnic society.
